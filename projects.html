Power BI

Cost Effectiveness Dashboard

This dashboard explores how hospital stay duration, discharge destination, and follow-up rates vary by age and gender. It provides insights into patient flow and resource use, which are key indicators of cost effectiveness in care.

My Role:
I used Power Query to clean and structure the data, and DAX to calculate average lengths of stay, follow-up percentages, and top diagnoses. The dashboard visualizes these patterns across gender and age brackets.

Real-World Use:
This tool can help hospital administrators identify high-cost diagnoses and adjust care pathways or discharge strategies to reduce unnecessary long stays and improve efficiency.

Department Effectiveness Dashboard

This dashboard compares department performance across age and gender, based on patient satisfaction scores and discharge outcomes.
The goal was to evaluate whether different patient groups experience consistent care quality across departments.

My Role:
I segmented the data using Power Query and created measures in DAX to visualize satisfaction levels and discharge destinations per department, filtered by gender and age group.

Real-World Use:
Hospitals can use this dashboard to benchmark departments, identify weaknesses in specific age or gender groups, and tailor training to improve communication, care delivery, and discharge planning.

Treatment Effectiveness Dashboard

This Power BI dashboard analyzes how patient characteristics — such as age and diagnosis — influence outcomes after hospital discharge.
The goal was to uncover patterns that affect recovery pathways and hospital planning.

My Role:
I used Power Query for data cleaning and transformation, then applied DAX to calculate adherence, follow-up, and discharge rates. The dashboard segments results by diagnosis and age group for clearer storytelling.

Real-World Use: These findings can help healthcare managers plan more effective care pathways, especially for chronic or elderly patients.
